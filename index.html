About Me

Hi, my name is Ranjan, and I am a full stack developer. I have a passion for building cross platform connectivity accross applications, and building responsive applications. I love solving problems and creating intuitive user experiences, and I am always looking for new ways to learn and grow in my field.

I am a 3rd year student (2024 batch), pursuing B.Tech CSE from VIT Bhopal. I have developed multiple project variying from websites to android applications to desktop games.

I am an experienced and driven problem solver, with quite some track record of success in hackathons. I have won some hackathons, including Hack GDSC (mobile track) and VITB Game Jam Season 1 with being a finalist in the NXP AIM Smart Car Race Challenge.

These experiences have not only honed my technical skills, but have also taught me the importance of teamwork and collaboration in acheiving success.I am confident that my expertise and passion for problem solving will make me a valuable asset to any team or project.
